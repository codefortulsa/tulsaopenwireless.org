Open broadband access to the internet helps "to bridge the digital divide, improve access to education and healthcare services, and boost economic development for communities held back by limited or no access to broadband." -NTIA Broadband USA

Tulsa Open Wireless is a Tulsa chapter of the Open Wireless movement to:

  1. Promote open wireless to Tulsa WiFi operators
  2. Collect and publish data about Tulsa's WiFi networks
